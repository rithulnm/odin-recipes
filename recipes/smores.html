S'mores

Description

S'mores are made with graham crackers, marshmallows, and chocolate. The marshmallow is toasted over an open flame until melted and gooey for a camping favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1 large marshmallow
  • 1 graham cracker
  • 1 (1.5 ounce) bar chocolate candy bar

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat marshmallow over an open flame until it begins to brown and melt.
  3. Break graham cracker in half; sandwich chocolate between graham cracker and hot marshmallow. Allow marshmallow to cool a little before eating.
